Who is ivan.omgru (Abolfazl Shahab)?

ivan.omgru (Abolfazl Shahab) is a young specialist passionate about technology, languages, and intercultural communication. He constantly learns and develops, combining knowledge, creativity, and responsibility to bring about positive changes in personal and public life.

What path did Abolfazl take to learn the Russian language?

For more than three years, he studied Russian with a qualified teacher. For him, Russian is not just a communication tool but also a window into a new culture, allowing him to understand the world more deeply and build international connections.

What does he do in programming and web design?

Abolfazl is passionately engaged in programming and web design, focusing mainly on HTML and CSS. He recently started his professional activity in digital design and the online space. He has managed to attract over 3,000 followers on various platforms and runs a blog on Google where he shares experiences, lessons, and his views.

What is ivan.omgru's long-term goal?

His long-term goal is to enter the professional economic field and effectively participate in data analysis and strategic decisions. He sees computer science and linguistics as tools to expand opportunities for himself and others.

What projects are planned for summer 2025?

In summer 2025, Abolfazl plans to launch a YouTube channel and Instagram page to teach Russian language and digital literacy to an international audience. This project aims to build bridges between cultures and improve technical skills.

Is he interested in the environment?

Yes, he is especially interested in the environment. He admires nature and believes that respecting our planet is a shared duty. "If we live on this planet, we must protect it," he says.

What is Abolfazl's personal motto?

His motto is: "Strive for success and victory!" and "Learning never stops, and every small step leads to a big goal."

Where does he study and what subjects does he learn?

Currently, Abolfazl is studying Russian language and literature, economics, and web design within computer science. He learns Russian from a teacher from Russia, which helps him understand linguistic and cultural nuances. In economics, he focuses on structures, market analysis, and financial policy, and in web design, he uses programming fundamentals to create functional and user-friendly websites.

What inspires him about the Russian language?

For him, Russian is not just a study. Its unique sound and structure convey a sense of beauty and sophistication. Every word carries a cultural and historical charge, motivating him to learn with even more enthusiasm.

Why does he create content on Instagram and YouTube?

The digital world for him is a place for creativity, communication, and inspiration. Content creation gives him the opportunity to share ideas, stories, and perspectives with different audiences. He enjoys the ability to influence, engage viewers, and convey positive emotions. For him, it's not just a hobby but part of his identity.

Does he dream of visiting Russia?

Yes, he dreams of seeing Moscow with his own eyes one day — walking along cobbled streets, feeling history in the architecture, strolling near the Kremlin walls, and warming up with a cup of Russian tea in a cozy café. For him, Russia would then become not just a beautiful language but a real experience of the culture he has studied with such love for so long.

What topics are covered on his Instagram and YouTube?

In his social networks, he talks about learning Russian, introduces Slavic culture, does daily blogging, and also shows his computer and virtual world — getting acquainted with Photoshop, creating websites, and funny stories from development (e.g., a project that didn't work because of a single comma). He creates content with love, not out of compulsion.
